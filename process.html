Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our unlock process

How long does the unlock process take?

With our Express Service, most devices are unlocked within 20 minutes. Standard Service takes 12-24 hours. Processing time depends on the device model and current server load.

Do I need to send my device to you?

No, our service is completely remote. We only require your device's IMEI or serial number to process the unlock. You'll receive instructions via email once the process is complete.

Is my personal data safe during the process?

Absolutely. We never access your personal data during the unlock process. Our method only communicates with Apple servers to remove the activation lock, without touching your personal information.

Will this process work on the latest iOS version?

Yes, our service supports all iOS versions including the latest releases. We continuously update our methods to ensure compatibility with new iOS updates.

How will I know when my device is unlocked?

You'll receive an email notification with confirmation and instructions once your device is unlocked. You can also check status through your account dashboard.
